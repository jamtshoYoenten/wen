Defination

Digital marketing, also called online marketing, is the promotion of brands to connect with potential customers using the internet and other forms of digital communication.

Mission

The main mission of a digital marketing project is to leverage online channels, platforms, and technologies to achieve specific marketing objectives and business goals.

The role of digital marketing is to help you garner new traffic, leads, and sales for your business by reaching people looking for your products and services.
